Things worth your team's attention:

The full-screen green "Collected" state likely auto-dismisses (it's gone by the time the operator reframes) — worth confirming the timeout value and whether it's interruptible by the next scan, or whether it blocks input during the animation.
The "Already Scanned" toast keeps the camera stream live, which is the right pattern — make sure this path doesn't re-POST to the backend on every detection of the same code (should be a client-side dedupe against the local scanned list, not a round-trip).
The group is at 100% but scanning still works. Confirm whether post-100% scans are (a) ignored, (b) treated as exceptions, or (c) silently accepted — the video only shows the duplicate case, not a net-new scan after 100%.
Footer says "Camera mode," which implies there's a toggle to hardware-scanner mode. Given the barcode scanner debugging thread on this project, worth a note to QA on whether both modes emit the same confirmation UI.
